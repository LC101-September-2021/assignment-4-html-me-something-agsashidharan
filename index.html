Nearby Hotels Are Overbooked and Flights to Nevada are Sold Out

The small towns near Area 51 were not prepared for the viral attention they suddenly started receiving. Small hotels started to become fully booked and overbooked for the dates surrounding the event. Flights to Nevada soon became reserved as millions of people were considering how they can attend the event. Lots of people thought of it as a funny idea to go there, but a some people actually did it.
